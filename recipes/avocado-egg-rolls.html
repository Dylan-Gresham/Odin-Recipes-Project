Avocado Egg Rolls

A Cheesecake Factory Copycat Recipe

Description

Avocado Egg Rolls are a delicious snack that can be made in a short amount of time, typically under an hour! These rolls consist of a fried, crispy exterior with a smoothy, flavor packed avocado mixture on the inside.

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 3 Avocados (diced)
  • 1/4 cup Red Onion (diced)
  • 8 tablespoons Sun Dried Tomato (cut into small pieces)
  • 3 tablespoons Cilantro leaves (chopped)
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Lime worth of juice
  • 1/2 of a Lemon's juice
  • Vegetable oil for frying, the amount needed will vary depending on your pot size
  • 8-12 Wonton wrappers
  • 1 egg (if doing more than one serving still use one egg and just add water to it)

Steps

  1. Begin by chopping up your avocados, red onion, sun dried tomatoes, and cilantro if not done already.
  2. Next add all of your chopped ingredients to a mixing bowl and mix until everything is incorporated and your avocado is no longer chunky.
  3. Once everything is mixed together, add in your lime juice and mix around a bit more.
  4. Prepare egg mix by cracking your 1 egg into a small bowl and adding a bit of water then mix it together until the yolk and whites are mixed together.
  5. Now, get a cutting board and place a wonton wrapper on it.
  6. Using your finger or a sauce brush, coat all four edges of the wonton wrapper with the egg wash. Make sure to get about 1/8th of an inch of each edge covered by the egg wash mixture so it can properly stick once folded up.
  7. Using a spoon or your mixing utensil, place some of your mixture on to the center of the wrapper in a line with each end of the line pointing to a corner.
  8. Fold the wrapper like a burrito by folding one of the corners that the line of mixture isn't pointing towards over the mixture, tucking the sides in, and then rolling up into a cylindrical shape.
  9. Set aside on a pan, plate, or wire rack until you're ready to fry.
  10. Repeat steps 5-9 until you're out of the avocado mixture or wonton wrappers.
  11. Once all your egg rolls are ready to be fried, add enough vegetable oil to a pot to where an egg roll could completely submerge in the oil without touching the bottom of the pot.
  12. Turn your stove on to High and wait for your oil to heat up.
  13. Add in as many egg rolls to the oil as you can without having the egg rolls touch each other. They should fry for about 2-3 minutes or until the outside of the wrapper is a nice golden brown color.
  14. Remove egg rolls from the oil once they're done frying and place onto either a wire rack with paper towels or a pan underneath to catch the oil that falls off or place directly on to paper towels.
  15. Repeat Steps 13 and 14 until all egg rolls are done frying.
  16. Optionally, cut each egg roll diagonally down the middle to plate and serve.
  17. Finally, enjoy!
